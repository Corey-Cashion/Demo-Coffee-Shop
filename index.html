Long Black

A "long black" is a style of coffee that originated in Australia and New Zealand. It is made by pouring a double shot of espresso or a concentrated amount of strong black coffee over hot water.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a popular espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy. It is traditionally composed of equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk. The result is a layered coffee drink with a rich espresso base, a smooth and creamy middle layer of steamed milk, and a frothy and airy top layer of foam.

$9

Classic Latte

A latte, short for caffè latte, is a popular espresso-based coffee drink. The word "latte" itself means "milk" in Italian. Lattes are known for their creamy consistency and a balance between the boldness of espresso and the sweetness of steamed milk.

Iced Coffee

Iced coffee is a chilled coffee beverage served over ice. It's a popular and refreshing alternative to hot coffee, especially in warmer weather. The preparation method involves brewing coffee and allowing it to cool before pouring it over ice.

$7

Iced Latte

An Iced Latte is a chilled coffee beverage that combines espresso, cold milk, and ice. It's a refreshing and creamy coffee option, perfect for those who enjoy the rich flavor of espresso with a cool and smooth finish. The preparation involves pulling a shot of espresso and then pouring it over a generous amount of ice. Cold milk is added to the espresso.

$9

Vuno Cold Brew

Our cold brew coffee is known for its smooth, mellow flavor, and low acidity. Unlike traditional hot brewed coffee, cold brew is made by steeping coarsely ground coffee beans in cold or room temperature water for an extended period. This slow extraction process results in a concentrated coffee liquid, which is diluted with spring water before sering.
